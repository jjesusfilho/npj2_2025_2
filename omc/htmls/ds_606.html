Summary of the dispute to date

The summary below was up-to-date at

Consultations

Complaint by Saudi Arabia

On 17 August 2021, Saudi Arabia requested consultations with the European Union with respect to the European Union’s provisional anti-dumping duty on imports of mono-ethylene glycol originating in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Saudi Arabia claimed that the measures at issue appear to be inconsistent with:

  • Articles 1, 2.1, 2.2, 2.2.1.1., 2.2.2, 2.3, 2.4, 3.1, 3.2, 3.3, 3.4, 3.5, 5.2, 5.3, 5.4, 5.8, 6.1, 6.5, 6.5.1, 6.8, and 18.1 and Annex II of the Anti-Dumping Agreement; and
     
  • Articles VI:1 and VI:2 of the GATT 1994.
